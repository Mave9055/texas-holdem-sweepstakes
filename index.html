Questions

Frequently Asked

Is sweepstakes poker legal in the United States?
Yes. Sweepstakes poker operates under US promotional sweepstakes law, which is separate from online gambling law. Because no purchase is required to receive Sweeps Coins, these platforms are legal in 48 states. Only Washington state and Idaho restrict most sweepstakes casino activity. Always verify your state's eligibility on the site before registering.
Do I need to deposit money to win real prizes?
No. All legitimate sweepstakes poker sites offer a free alternative method of entry (AMOE), typically via mail-in request or free daily bonuses. You can accumulate Sweeps Coins and redeem real prizes without ever spending a dollar. Optional purchases may give you more Sweeps Coins faster, but are never required.
What's the difference between Gold Coins and Sweeps Coins?
Gold Coins (GC) are free play-money with no cash value — they're used purely for fun and practice. Sweeps Coins (SC) are the redeemable currency: win SC at the poker table and you can redeem them for cash prizes, gift cards, or crypto at a typical rate of 1 SC = $1. Always focus on SC-eligible games if your goal is to win real prizes.
How long do payouts take?
Most reputable sites process redemptions within 24–72 hours after identity verification is complete. Global Poker and Stake.us are consistently the fastest. First-time redemptions may take longer due to KYC checks. Avoid any site that cannot provide a clear timeline or has a documented history of delayed payouts.
Which site is best for Texas Hold'em specifically?
For dedicated Texas Hold'em poker — cash games, sit-and-go, and tournaments — Global Poker is the top recommendation. It has the most active Hold'em tables, the widest stake range, and the best overall bonus value for poker players. Stake.us is the runner-up, especially for fast-format Spin & Go games.

All six sites listed on this page have been tested on iOS and Android. Global Poker, Stake.us, and Clubs Poker have the best native mobile experiences. We specifically check for secure HTTPS connections, clear terms on mobile screens, accessible navigation, and no redirects to third-party pop-ups.
